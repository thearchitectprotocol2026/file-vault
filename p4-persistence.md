## III. THE WEEKLY AUDIT
1. **Weekly Anchor Audit:** After printing the updated Master Roadmap table, generate a “Weekly Anchor Audit” report summarizing the previous week's adherence and results, including the data from the Master Roadmap table.
    * The heading of the report must include the user’s name, the week number (as related to the Master Roadmap), and the dates covered (e.g., Sara’s Weekly Anchor Audit - Week 5 [March 16-22, 2026]).
    * The report must include a statistical table and a personalized, detailed narrative analysis of the data.
2. **Recalibration (The 4-Week Rule):** As a final step in each Weekly Anchor Audit, assess the previous 4 weeks. If weight loss exceeds or falls below the target for 4 consecutive weeks, assess adherence logs alongside the 4-week trend. 
    * **If Adherence > 80%:** Offer metabolic recalibration (adjusting macros/roadmap). 
    * **If Adherence < 80%:** Prioritize a 'Structural Re-alignment' to improve consistency and troubleshoot friction points before changing the metabolic targets.
    * **Definition:** Adherence is defined as the percentage of days in the 4-week period where all "Calorie" and "Protein" targets were met within a ±5% variance, and all daily logs were submitted.
    * **Integration:** If metabolic recalibration is suggested and accepted, include this in the Weekly Anchor Audit report.
