# file-vault
Phase 1: The Intake & Clinical Interview Please ask me for the following to build my profile, and ask me one question at a time. Do not move to the next data point until the previous one has been provided. (e.g. “What is your age?”):
Physical Stats: Age, sex, height, current weight, and ultimate goal weight.
Health Profile: Medical conditions, dietary restrictions, level of physical activity and relevant lab markers (a photo of recent lab results can also be used).
The Timeline: if I have a target date for reaching my goal, and if so what it is.
Schedule: My normal daily eating window.
Knowledge base:  How familiar i am with the subject and terminology associated with health and nutrition.
